My Story

I always knew I had a knack for baking. Growing up in a Mexican household, I was frequently tasked with kitchen duties. That fortunately included baking! From following recipes passed down through generations to crafting my own abominations (but delicious), I have mastered the art of baking. Now, I want to share my passion with others and hope to inspire those that are interested in baking. Remember, the sweetest hearts create the sweetest treats!
